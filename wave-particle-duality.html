Understanding Wave-Particle Duality

Explore the fundamental concepts that revolutionized our understanding of quantum mechanics.

Wave Nature

Light exhibits wave-like properties such as interference, diffraction, and polarization. These phenomena can only be explained if light behaves as a wave.

Particle Nature

Light also behaves as discrete packets of energy called photons. The photoelectric effect and Compton scattering demonstrate this particle-like behavior.

The Dual Nature

Quantum mechanics reveals that all quantum entities possess both wave and particle characteristics, with the observed behavior depending on the experimental setup.
