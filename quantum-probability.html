Electron Probability Cloud

In quantum mechanics, we can't say exactly where an electron is - only where it's likely to be. This creates a "probability cloud" around the atom.

What Are Probability Clouds?

Unlike planets that follow predictable orbits, electrons exist as probability distributions. The electron cloud shows where we're most likely to find an electron if we look for it.
